## What is Crew?

Crew is an **AI-powered task management platform** that brings AI coding agents to everyone on your team. Whether you're an engineer, PM, or business stakeholder, Crew helps you plan, review, and safely execute AI-assisted coding tasks. Each task runs in an isolated git worktree, giving you complete control over your codebase whilst leveraging the power of AI assistants.

## Why Crew?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Safe AI Execution" icon="shield-check">
    Every task runs in an isolated git worktree. Agents can't interfere with each other or your main branch.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Team Collaboration" icon="users">
    Enable non-technical team members to leverage AI agents. Engineers, PMs, and business teams can all work together with AI-powered task management.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Visual Code Review" icon="code-compare">
